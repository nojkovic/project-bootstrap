Animal welfare and care

An animal is in a good state of well-being when it is healthy, fed, safe, able to display behaviors important to its physical and mental condition, if it is comfortable and when it does not suffer from unpleasant conditions such as pain, fear and stress.Good welfare includes disease prevention and adequate veterinary care, shelter provision, nutrition, a stimulating and safe environment.
